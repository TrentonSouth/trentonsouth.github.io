Target Audience

The target audience for unlimited-weather.net is described below:

  • All users who are able to read and follow simple prompt instructions.
  • 6+
  • Home computer with internet connection.
  • This site is users of all income ranges.
  • To know how to plan their day and the days to come in regard to weather restrictions.
